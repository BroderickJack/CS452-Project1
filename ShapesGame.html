Wack-a-Shape

Rules 

- You must click the shapes that match the target shape in the black box 

    - Clicking the shape faster will result in a higher score 

    - There is a 50% reduction in awarded points for clicking the correct shape but wrong color 

    - You will earn (-) points for clicking the incorrect color and incorrect shape 

    - There is a (-20) point penalty for not clicking the background 

- In order to win you must earn (+)1000 Points 

- Scoring (-)500 points will be an automatic loss
